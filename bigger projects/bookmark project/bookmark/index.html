Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions you'd like answered please feel free to email us.

What is Bookmark?

Bookmark is a clean and simple extension that helps you organize your favorite websites. When you open a new browser tab, you'll instantly see all your most-visited sites in one place, making it faster and easier to access the pages you use every day.

How can I request a new browser?

We're always looking to expand browser support. If you'd like to see Bookmark available on a specific browser, send us your request through our contact form or email us directly. We prioritize new browser support based on user demand, so your feedback really matters.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, Bookmark is available as a browser extension for desktop browsers. We're exploring mobile options for the future. If you'd like to see a mobile version, let us know—it helps us understand what our users want most.

What about other Chromium browsers?

Great news! Bookmark works with all Chromium-based browsers, including Edge, Brave, Opera, Vivaldi, and others. If it runs on Chromium, you can install Bookmark from the Chrome Web Store and it should work seamlessly.Retry
